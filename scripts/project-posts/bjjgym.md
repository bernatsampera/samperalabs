## Project Overview

BJJGym.com is a comprehensive platform for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioners to discover training facilities while traveling. The platform connects BJJ enthusiasts with gyms, open mats, and training partners worldwide, ensuring they never miss a training session.

## The Challenge

BJJ travelers often struggle to find reliable information about academies when visiting new cities. Existing solutions lacked comprehensive gym details, verification systems, and user-friendly interfaces. The challenge was to create a trusted resource that serves both travelers and gym owners.

### AI Integration Highlights

Each gym profile on BJJGym.com is enhanced through sophisticated AI processing:

- AI-powered analysis of unstructured gym data from various sources to create structured, consistent gym profiles
- Natural language processing to extract key information from reviews and testimonials
- Automated verification system that cross-references information across multiple sources to ensure accuracy
- Personalized gym recommendations based on a user's training history, belt level, and preferences

## Key Features

- **Global Gym Directory:** Comprehensive database of BJJ academies across 150+ countries.
- **Open Mat Finder:** Discover drop-in friendly training sessions while traveling.
- **Training Partner Network:** Connect with fellow practitioners based on weight class, experience level, and training goals.
- **Gym Owner Dashboard:** Tools for academy owners to showcase their facilities and attract visitors.
- **Review System:** Verified feedback from community members who have trained at the gyms.

## Results

Since launching BJJGym.com:

- Platform now features 2,000+ verified gyms across 1,000+ cities
- Monthly open mat sessions exceeding 150 worldwide
- Active user base of 200+ BJJ practitioners from 30+ countries
